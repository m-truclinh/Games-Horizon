Overview

Game's Horizon was founded by 3 young gaming enthusiasts who wanted to bring the best contents about gaming and everything related to this topic. We as a team felt like something was missing when we were just readers and consumers, something that other gaming newsletters couldn't fill. So we started our own which covers E-sports, platform games, mobile games and also news about Mainstream game developers as well as Indie developers and their games. We love all games from Ego-Shooters to MMORPGs and Mobile Games, that is why we started this company in the first place so that gamers can enjoy not only mainstream but also games which deserves more attention even with a low budget. We are gamers at heart and we hope that you are too.
